Gucci

Gucci started with saddlery shop in France in 1906 that began with leather bags specifically for horsemen. It slowly took its progrss and later on luxurious luggages are made together with the evolution of transportation.

In 1938, the first retail shop of Gucci opened in Via Condotti, Rome. Gucci immediately became recognized for its leather bag with a bamboo handle that was followed by the other classics like luggage, ties, shoes and that completely dominated the industry. Today, Gucci is recognized for its high quality products ranging from bags, shoes, apparel, cosmetics and even fragrance.
