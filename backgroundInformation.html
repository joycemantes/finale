BACKGROUND INFORMATION

Mary Joyce Mantes is a filipina that was born in March 17,1992. She took Humanities and Social Science during her senior education as she had a strong attraction towards literature, she was one of the most actives in school given that she participates with competitions; she graduated with honors. She then proceeded to take Bachelor of Science in Information Technology as her college degree and became a consistent President's lister; later on, she graduated with the title of Summa Cum Laude.

After living in Philippines for 18 years, she decided to study in New York where she was accepted by a Fashion School. Later on, her impressive skills was discovered by a fashion organization that supported her ideas until she was able to establish her own brand; Gucci.
